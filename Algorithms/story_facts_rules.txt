# Facts
Macbeth killed Duncan.
Lady Macbeth convinced Macbeth to kill Duncan.
Banquo was killed by Macbeth's hired murderers.
Macduff eventually killed Macbeth.
Macbeth became the king of Scotland.

# Rules
Macbeth killed Duncan -> Macbeth is guilty of regicide.
Macduff killed Macbeth -> Macduff is the new hero of Scotland.
